About us
This is a telemedicine facility aimed at providing quality healthcare at the comfort of the patient.It was initiated in 2024 by Sally Muthoni and Joy Karwitha for the purpose of improving healthcare.NovaMediX is a groundbreaking mobile application designed to bridge the gap between healthcare services and underserved populations, especially in remote areas. It empowers individuals by offering easy access to essential medical features directly from their mobile devices, eliminating geographical barriers and making healthcare more inclusive.
